Keep the Future Human: Survival RPG

Scope:
This text-based survival RPG immerses players in a near-future world shaped by the choices humanity faces around artificial intelligence. Inspired by Anthony Aguirre’s Keep the Future Human, the game challenges you to balance innovation, governance, and ethics to ensure that technology remains a tool for empowerment — not replacement.

You will interact with governments, corporations, scientists, and civil society, navigating competing interests while preserving human agency in the age of accelerating AI. Your survival depends on making strategic, moral, and cooperative choices.

Can you keep the future human?
